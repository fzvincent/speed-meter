Our app requests access to your device's GPS location to calculate speed and distance. This data:

  • ✅ Is NOT shared with third parties
  • ✅ Is used only for speed calculation

Usage Analytics

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app, including:

  • Page views and user interactions

  • Performance metrics

We use:
